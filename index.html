About Delou

Redefining the modern bathroom through a classical lens.

Delou is a design-led bathroom atelier born from a simple observation: while every other room in the home has evolved into a curated sanctuary, the bathroom remains trapped in clinical monotony. The market is dominated by white glazed porcelain — sterile, uninspired, and fundamentally disconnected from how we actually live.

We set out to fill this void. Our collections draw on the rich heritage of European porcelain craft, reimagined for contemporary life. Each piece is produced by master artisans and arrives in colours that the industry abandoned decades ago: deep chocolate, heritage green, burgundy, warm sand.

Where established brands prioritise mass-manufacturing efficiency over soul, Delou treats every fixture as an architectural statement. Our toilets, basins, and bathtubs carry the proportions and detailing of vintage design, paired with the performance and plumbing standards expected today.

Starting from Stockholm and serving the Nordics, we work with architects, interior designers, and private homeowners who believe the bathroom deserves the same intention as every other room.

For those who refuse the ordinary.

Delou Studio

Porcelain in colour for the spaces that matter.

Working with architects, designers, and developers.

We partner with architects, interior designers, and property developers who understand that the bathroom is not an afterthought — it is architecture. Delou Studio exists to bring coloured porcelain into the spaces they create: hotels, restaurants, residences, and beyond.

The bathroom market has been dominated by white glazed porcelain for decades. Sterile, uninspired, and fundamentally disconnected from how we actually live. While every other room in the home has evolved into a curated sanctuary, the bathroom has been left behind.

We set out to change that. Our collections draw on the rich heritage of European porcelain craft, reimagined for contemporary life. Each piece is produced by master artisans and arrives in colours that the industry abandoned long ago: deep chocolate, heritage green, burgundy, warm sand.

These are not decorative novelties. They are architectural fixtures — designed with vintage proportions and the detailing of an earlier era, paired with the performance and plumbing standards expected today.

The bathroom has been white for too long. We exist to change that.

For hospitality projects, we work closely with your design team to develop a bathroom identity that reinforces the narrative of your space. A boutique hotel in Stockholm. A members' club in Copenhagen. A restaurant in Oslo. The bathroom should not break the spell — it should deepen it.

For residential projects, we consult on fixture selection, colour specification, and spatial composition. Whether it is a private home, an apartment, or an estate — we ensure the bathroom carries the same architectural intention as every other room.

For property developers building at scale, we offer volume supply and specification support. Differentiate your projects with bathroom environments that buyers and guests remember long after they leave.

Our range includes console and pedestal basins, close-coupled and high cistern toilets, and freestanding bathtubs — all available across our full colour palette. Every fixture is designed to be specified together, creating complete, cohesive bathroom environments.

We are always seeking new collaborations and projects. If you are developing a space that deserves more than white porcelain, we would love to hear from you.
